Hélène Von Or

Hélène Von Or is a luxury fashion brand, launched in year 2015. The brand is a product of dreams and inspirations, carried by the designers from their early years. Main idea behind this barnd is to balance haute couture and pret-a-porter. The designs are created in consideration of fashionforward mood and timeless vision. Each design embraces femininity, while mixing edge with classy. "Fashion as a lifestyle" is a brand's point of view.
